History

Starting around 2005, due to the resource boom initiated by Western Australia, the Korean population in WA began to increase rapidly. Since 2013, exchange students and some church members have joined the Bull Creek Tennis Club as members. Starting at the end of that year, they began a separate gathering for Korean compatriots on Saturday nights. Kim Se-young (current coach), Kim Dae-yeon (inaugural captain), Kim Ki-nam (inaugural treasurer), Lee Seung-mo (inaugural advisor), Jeon Chang-yeol (inaugural president), Lee Jae-jin, and others participated.

Presidents

1st President

Jeon Chang-yeol 2013 - 2017

KTC began its journey with Baekyang Church Tennis Association at Willetton tennis court, but as the inaugural president, Jeon successfully negotiated court and clubhouse usage agreement with Bull Creek Tennis Club. Additionally, Jeon established the club's regulations, achieved success in overall club management, organized the first club championship.

2nd President

Bae Hyomin 2018 - 2021

As the second president, Hyomin successfully hosted major tournaments every year despite a somewhat subdued atmosphere due to a decrease in new members during the pandemic-induced border closures. He made consistent efforts for the advancement of KTC and continues to actively engage in donation activities for the club's development anonymously.

3rd President

Kim Tae-kyun 2022 - 2023

As the third president, Tae-kyun breathed new life into the club, seamlessly organizing the most successful quarterly tournaments in its history. Demonstrating his leadership skills to such an extent that he received the 2023 WA Leadership Award, Tae-kyun led to the highest-ever rate of membership participation. Tae-kyun successfully initiated the first-ever Burselton Club MT.

4th President

Kim Byeong-gu 2024 -

Byeong-gu's passion for tennis is incomparable to anyone else's. He always seeks competitive games and accurately assesses the skills of members with sharp insight. It is expected that he will make significant contributions to the club by leveraging his strengths.
